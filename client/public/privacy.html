Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 23, 2025

Local Online Compiler is an ad-supported, browser-based code runner. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Code and inputs: Code you type stays in your browser by default. We send code and STDIN to our execution API only when you click Run.
  • Log data: We collect basic diagnostics (IP, user agent, timestamps, error codes) to prevent abuse and improve stability.
  • Ads: Google AdSense may set cookies or use local storage to deliver and measure ads. See Google's policy.

How we use data

  • Provide code execution, rate limiting, and security monitoring.
  • Improve performance and fix bugs through aggregated analytics.
  • Show contextual ads that keep the free tier available.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not permanently store your code unless you export it or opt into future sync features.
  • We do not show ads on empty, under-construction, or navigation-only pages.

Cookies and consent

AdSense may use cookies for frequency capping, fraud prevention, and ad measurement. You can manage cookies through your browser settings. If you block cookies, the site will still run, but ads may be less relevant.

Data retention

Execution logs are kept for up to 30 days for abuse prevention. Aggregated analytics may be retained longer without personal identifiers.

Contact

Email support@localcompiler.app with privacy questions or to request access or deletion of any data we hold about you.
